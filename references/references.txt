The dataset comprises information on penguins with the following features:
- Species: Categorization of penguin species (e.g., Adelie).
- Island: The geographical location or island where the penguin was observed.
- CulmenLength: The length of the penguin's bill (culmen).
- CulmenDepth: The depth of the penguin's bill (culmen).
- FlipperLength: The length of the penguin's flipper.
- BodyMass: The body mass of the penguin.
- Sex: Gender of the penguin (e.g., MALE or FEMALE).
- Year: The year the observation was recorded.
